Research

My long-term research interest is to find a path to build a universal learning system, and my short-term research interest is to explore a better path to build universal visual perception system. Now, I am closely collaborating with Han Hu, Yue Cao and Steve Lin at Microsoft Research Asia. Previously, I closely collaborated with Xizhou Zhu. Meanwhile, I was mentored by Prof. Xiang Bai as a student at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, and by Jifeng Dai as a young researcher at Microsoft Research Asia.

Please drop me an email if you are interested in doing research with me.
